What is this?


Across the globe, 617 million children are missing basic math and reading skills. Many children loose track of school and have no one to lean on. If we give every child the opportunity to learn this could change the course of their lives.


Volunteacher is a project for a nonprofit educational app that will connects learners who need to get help with what they're studying at school with tutors who are interested in teaching underprivileged children anywhere in the world for free.

How it works?

The app helps students find tutors who match their learning needs with same language spoken.

Learners can have online tutoring.

Khan Academy is the best tool for tutors with their teaching.

Students will fill in gaps in their understanding and then learn to practice at their own.

This project was created at Le Wagon workshop.
